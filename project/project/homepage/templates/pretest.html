Test Overview

IQ tests measure a variety of different types of abilities such as

  • Verbal
  • Mathematical
  • Spatial
  • Reasoning Skills

At the end of the exam, the results will be shown based on: correct answers and test's total consumed time. Remember that your accuracy on responses is more important than the total time taken.

Avoid Use

During this test it is not possible to use pencil, paper or calculator. Challenge your brain.

Mixed Areas of Evaluation

This exam will evaluate several areas like math, verbal, spatial creation and logic thinking. A little from everything.

Limited Time

You will have to answer 20 questions as quick and accurate as possible. The maximum time to finish the test is 20 min. Be precise.

Take in mind that there are other characteristics such as outstanding artistic, creativeness, communication that when combined with personal aptitudes such as ambition, good temperament and compassion, could result in an outstanding level of success despite a low measured IQ"
