What is an IQ Test

It is a standardized test that is used to evaluate your abilities of fast comprehension under timed conditions and for some educators and psychologists it also helps to predict the academic performance of an individual as well as to detect several learning disabilities. This kind of test is like a psychometric test, they attempt to quantify important aspects of a human being like its personality, attitude, intelligence and emotion because the overcome of that number will reveal the extent of a person's ability to think.

Results are interesting...

Your score reflects your average ability to navigate new and unfamiliar environments. The average IQ score is comprised of scores from many avenues of assessment like emotional intelligence, visuospacial abilities, verbal, cognitive reasoning, and mathematical understanding, to name a few.
